Welcome to the Tiger Program Readiness Questionnaire

Karate Atlanta’s Tiger Program is the result of over 25 years of working with young students and families. It was created specifically for children ages 4 to 6 and is backed by the experience of the American Taekwondo Association, founded in 1969.

Our goal isn’t just to teach kicks and punches — it’s to help your child build the focus, confidence, respect, coordination, and classroom habits they’ll need for school and life.
